Welcome

Hello and welcome to the food truck of your dreams newest website, where you can order food and pick it up at the closets food truck near you.

Oxtail Your Way a family run foodtruck. which has started up from a dream that a hispanic father has made, after working as Chef for years and finding his passion in cooking. Starting as a dishwasher in buffets then to getting a job at Montifiore and handing out food to then becoming the head chef who only cooks for higher ups and known as one of the highest paid workers in the kitchen, came to his kids with a idea to open up a food truck, and specialize in his favorite food that his grandma introduced to him, OXTAIL!
